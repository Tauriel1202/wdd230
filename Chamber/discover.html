Discover Wayu-Wayu

Welcome to the Island of Wayu-Wayu! The island has a population of 2,000 people. The island is a popular destination for tourists for relaxing, surfing, and exploring. It is a great place for vacationing.

Wayu-Wayu boasts calm beaches great for tide-pooling, relaxing, swimming, and snorkeling. 🤿

Along with its calmer waters, Wayu-Wayu also presents some excellent beaches for wave-lovers. The wave heights range from 3ft-8ft depending on the location. 🌊

A dormant volcano rests in the center of the island. It overlooks all the island. Special tours are offered to those interested in climbing it. 🗻

Fueled by the underground warmth, hot springs surround the volcano on three sides. The temperatures range from 80℉ to 100℉, perfect for relaxing in. ♨

Beyond the soft sandy beaches, a tropical forest springs up. It surrounds the hot springs and the volcano. Tours are available on most days. 🌳

Along with its varied landscape, the island is home to a wide array of creatures. Butterflies, birds, lizards, and other animals can be found here. 🦎

Flowers adorned the island's forests, beaches, and cliffs. They are a part of the island's culture as well as its wildlife. 🌺

Fruits grow abundantly here. 🥥Coconut palms grow on the beaches. Pineapples grow on the edge of the forest. Dragonfruits and more populate the island's forest. 🍍

The island also enjoys a fun culture of music, festival, fruit, and more! 🌴
