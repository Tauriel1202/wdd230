Target Audience

Description of Target Audience

The target audience is mostly families and couples. They are looking for relaxation and/or outdoor activites. Income varies but is generally low-medium. Technology is mostly phones but should also be compatible with laptops.

Personas

A family looking for a nice island vacation. They have two kids ages 12 and 14. The whole family loves outdoor activites and is staying a week in the late summer. They have an average income. They will probably use their phones to access this site.

A couple is newly married and on their honeymoon. They are excited to visit a tropical island together. During their two weeks here in the late spring, they plan to relax. They have low income and are both still in school. They could use either a laptop or a phone.

A large family of six is looking a vacation with their kids. The kids are 14, 12, 5. The twins (age 5) are very energetic and love water. In the middle of the summer, the family will stay for a month. They are relatively low income, but they have been saving up for a while. They will probably use a laptop.
